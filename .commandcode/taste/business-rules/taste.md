# business-rules
- Campaign-relative week numbering (S01, S02, ...) starting from campaign start date (September 1), not ISO weeks. Confidence: 0.85
- Water invoice totals must be allocated by kg produced in each period (partes diarios first, then ventas/manual), never by days. Confidence: 0.85
- Past campaigns must only show monthly full-campaign view; no weekly/daily granularity. Confidence: 0.80
- Use billing period (e.g., "Marzo-Abril") as allocation window for water invoices, not meter reading dates. Confidence: 0.85
- Two periods with different kg must produce different water consumption amounts; equal consumption across unequal kg is invalid. Confidence: 0.80
- Past campaign filter UI must not show "últimas 6 semanas" or similar weekly selectors. Confidence: 0.80
- Merge functions for imported invoice/sales rows must replace persisted equivalents (by reference/m3/tipo_base+kg), not just skip duplicates. Confidence: 0.80
- Daily water meter readings (fuente: "contador", referencia: "agua-contador-general") replace invoice kg-based allocation for that day; remaining invoice liters are spread only across days without a meter reading. Confidence: 0.85
- Gasoil invoices are purchase events, not period consumption; liters must be spread proportionally across kg produced from the purchase date until the next gasoil purchase. Confidence: 0.85
- Water consumption unit must always be liters (l), never m3; input accepts Spanish decimal format (1.234,5). Confidence: 0.85
- Carga y descarga (loading/unloading) workers do not count for kg/persona calculation in Asistencia. Confidence: 0.70
- General daily T/h speed must be calculated as total kg / 1000 / (operational days * 8 hours), not weighted by producer/lote recorded hours. Confidence: 0.65
- Asistencia weekly comparison export should show average people per day, not accumulated total people over the period. Confidence: 0.65
- Produccion evolution chart in Analisis Diario must be hidden when there is only a single data point; show compact summary card instead. Confidence: 0.70
